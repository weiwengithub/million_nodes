Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

The "tabs" permission is used solely to read the titles and URLs of open browser tabs in order to display and manage them within the extension interface.

No data is transmitted to any external server. All functionality runs locally in your browser.

This extension does not use analytics or any third-party tracking services.
